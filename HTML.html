About Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios, first released in 2011. Players explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world, gathering resources, building structures, and surviving against monsters. Minecraft World Key Features

Key Features

  • Creative Mode: Build anything with unlimited resources.
  • Survival Mode: Gather resources, craft items, and fend off enemies.
  • Multiplayer: Play with friends on servers or local networks.
  • Modding: Customize the game with community-created mods.
